This group project, completed as part of my end-of-semester SAE in the first year of the BUT MMI program, involved reinventing a daily object by developing an innovative concept, a strong brand strategy, and a striking digital presence.

This is how SpeakLens was born: translator glasses designed for tourists, students, and professionals on the move. Prescription or sunglasses capable of displaying translations directly on the lenses, while remaining stylish and practical.

I was responsible for the complete design of the showcase website using WordPress. Despite my initial reservations with the CMS, I managed to leverage advanced plugins to structure and enrich the site. I am proud of the result, both functionally and visually.
